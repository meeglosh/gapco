Who We Are

An umbrella built for bold, original ideas.

GAPCO, The Great American Pineapple Company, is a venture studio and holding company for a growing portfolio of distinct brands. We build things that are considered, crafted, and a little unexpected.

From tactile, beautifully designed jigsaw puzzles to curated fashion goods to music experiences, every GAPCO venture starts with a simple question: what would we actually want to use?

Headquartered in the United States. Shipping ideas to the world.

How We Think

Build things worth keeping.

The pineapple has long been a symbol of welcome, warmth, and something a little out of the ordinary. That's the spirit we bring to everything we make.

We're not chasing trends. We're building brands that earn a permanent spot on the shelf, in the bag, in the daily routine.

01

Intentional Design

Every product we ship has a reason for existing. We obsess over the details so our customers don't have to wonder.

02

Built to Last

GAPCO ventures are built with longevity in mind, not flash-in-the-pan moments, but enduring brands that compound over time.

03

Distinctly Original

We don't copy the market. We look for the gap, the thing that should exist but doesn't yet, and we build it ourselves.
